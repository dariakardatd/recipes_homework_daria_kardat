Salads

Russian Salad

What will you need to cook a proper Russian salad (and not that mayo thing, you call a Russian salad)

  • - Fresh cucumber - 1 big
  • - Pickles - 1 jar
  • - Ham - 1 kg
  • - Boiled eggs - 10
  • - Boiled Potatoe - 3
  • - Green peas (canned and soft)
  • - Boiled carrot - 1
  • - Mayonnaise on your taste (I normally put about 300 grams)
  • - Pepper on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Boil eggs.
  2. 2. Boil potatoes and carrot. You can boil them together.
  3. 3. Chop ham, eggs, potatoes, carrot, cucumber and pickles.
  4. 4. Put all the ingredients into a big plate.
  5. 5. Add mayo, salt and pepper.
  6. 6. Mix everything.
  7. 7. Let is stay over a night or 8 hours.

Voilà! You have a traditional Russian salad. Enjoy!

Dressed herring

One more interesting traditional Russian salad. I bet you think it's a cake.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Boiled beet root - 1

  • - Boiled potatoe - 1

  • - Marinated herring - 200 grams

How to cook it:

  4. 4. Put mayo on all the sides.
  5. 5. Let is stay over a night or 8 hours.

Voilà! You have a traditional Dressed. Enjoy!

Soups

Borscht

A traditional Russian soup known all over the world.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Beef - 500 grams
  • - Potatatoes - 4
  • - Onion - 1
  • - Carrot - 1
  • - Beet root - 3
  • - Cabbage - 1 small head
  • - Garlic - 4 heads
  • - Tomato paste - 2 table spoons
  • - Vinegar - 1 table spoon
  • - Water - 3/4 litters
  • - Pepper on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Boil the beef.
  2. 2. Add pilled and cut in cubes potatoe to the boiled beef.
  3. 3. While you wait for potatoe to boil, chop and fry onion and carrot till golden color.
  4. 4.Add chopped garlick. Let it fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. 5. Add beet root and the vinegar. Fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. 6. Add tomato paste and fry for 5-7 minutes.
  7. 7. Put everything in the boiled beef and potatoes.
  8. 8. Add cabage and boil 7-10 minutes.
  9. 9. Add salt and pepper.

Voilà! Your traditional Borscht is ready. Enjoy!

Ukha

A traditional Russian fish soup.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Fish (any you like) - 1 kg
  • - Potatatoes - 2
  • - Onion - 1
  • - Carrot - 1
  • - Garlic - 2 heads
  • - Water - 3/4 litters
  • - Pepper ans spices on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Boil the fish with onion together. Ps. if you cook from red fish add half of the lemon.
  2. 2. Add pilled and cut in cubes potatoe to the boiled fish.
  3. 3. While you wait for potatoe to boil, chop and fry carrot till it's ready.
  4. 4. Add chopped garlick. Let it fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. 5. Once the fish is ready, take out the boiled onion.
  6. 6. Put everything in the boiled fish and potatoes.
  7. 7. Wait till it the water starts to boil.
  8. 8. Add salt and pepper.

Voilà! Your traditional Ukha is ready. Enjoy!

Second dishes

Captain meat

A traditional Russian dish cooked of beef and lots of cheese.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Beef - 1 kg
  • - Potatatoes - 5
  • - Parmesan cheese - 150 grams
  • - Mozzarella cheese - 150 grams
  • - Garlic - 2/3 heads
  • - Mayonnaise or yogurt on your taste
  • - Pepper on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Cut beef in steaks.
  2. 2. Cut potatoes on round slices.
  3. 3. Pill both cheeses.
  4. 4. Switch on oven for 180 degrees.
  5. 5. Put potatoes as a first layer on a backing sheet.
  6. 6. Put beef as a second layer.
  7. 7. Mix cheese, garlic and mayonnaise/yogurt.
  8. 8. Put cheese on top of beef as a third layer.
  9. 9. Add salt and pepper.
  10. 10. Put in the oven for 20 minutes or till cheese gets backed.

Voilà! Your traditional Captain meat is ready. Enjoy!

Meatballs

Traditional Russian meatballs.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Pork - 500 grams
  • - Onion - 1
  • - Egg - 1
  • - Bread white - 150 grams
  • - Wheat flower for breading
  • - Pepper on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Make minced meat from beef and pork. Mix them.

  3. 3. Edd egg.
  4. 4. Edd bread white.
  5. 5. Add salt and pepper for your taste. Mix everything.
  6. 6. Make meat balls.
  7. 7. Bread them in the wheat flower.
  8. 8. Fry.

Voilà! Your traditional Meatballs are ready. Enjoy!

Cabbage rolls

Traditional Cabbage rolls.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Beef - 250 grams
  • - Pork - 250 grams
  • - Tomatoes - 300 grams
  • - Rice - 150 grams
  • - Bouillon or water - 1 litter
  • - Cabbage - 1 head
  • - Carrot - 1
  • - Onion - 1
  • - Pepper on your taste
  • - Salt on your taste

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Make minced meat from beef and pork. Mix them.
  2. 2. Chop carrot and onion.Fry together till golden color.
    Add garlick and fry 2-3 minutes more.
  3. 3. Boil rice.
  4. 4. Mix minced meat, rice and fried vegetables.
  5. 5. Disassemble the cabbage into leaves.
    Put the mix minced meat with vegetables on the leaves.
    6. Rool cabbage rolls.
  6. 7. Fry rolls in a frying pan.
  7. 8. Fried rolls put into a pan, add bouillon/water and tomatoes.Stew under the cover for 40-60 minutes.

Voilà! Your traditional Cabbage rolls are ready. Enjoy!

Desserts

Blini

A traditional Russian dessert. Usually served for breakfast.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Eggs - 2
  • - Sour milk/yogurt - 250 grams
  • - Boiled water - 250 grams
  • - Wheat flower - 400 grams +/-
  • - Sugar - 1 table spoon
  • - Salt - 1/4 tea spoon
  • - Butter - around 20 grams

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Boil water.
  2. 2. Mix eggs with milk/yogurt.
  3. 3. Add boiled water.
  4. 4. Add salt and sugar.
  5. 5. Add wheat flower. Check the the consistency of the mass.
    must be thick, but pouring.
  6. 6. Heat the frying pan.
  7. 7. Take a full ladle of the mass and pour it on the frying pan. Try to make a circle.
  8. 8. Fry the blin from both sides till golden color.
  9. 9. Once it's ready put it on a plate and put a small piece of butter on top.
  10. 10. Reapeat with each one.

Voilà! Your traditional Russian Blini are ready. Serve it with honey pr jam or whatever you like. It's good with everything. Enjoy!

Rum ball

A traditional Russian dessert. Usually served for breakfast.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Shortbread - 300 grams
  • - Canned sweet milk - half can
  • - Butter - 100 grams
  • - Cacao - 2 table spoon

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Grind shortbread.
  2. 2. Mix canned sweet milk and butter.
  3. 3. Mix grind shortbread and the sweet mass.
  4. 4. Put cacao on a plate, add a peace of the dough paste and mold it in a shape of a sausage.
  5. 5. Reapeat with each one.
  6. 6. Once all molded, put in the fridge at least for 2 hours.

Voilà! Your traditional Rum Balls are ready. Enjoy!

Oladyi

A traditional Russian dessert. Usually served for breakfast.

To cook it you will need:

  • - Wheat flower - 240 grams
  • - Kefir - 250 grams
  • - Egg - 1
  • - Sugar - 2 table spoons
  • - Salt on your taste
  • - Oil - 3 table spoons

How to cook it:

  1. 1. Mix eggs with sugar and salt.
  2. 2. Warm up a bit the kefir.
  3. 3. Mix egg mass with warm kefir.
  4. 4. Mix the mass with wheat flower.
  5. 5. With a table spoon put the mass on the frying pan.
  6. 6. Fry from both sides till golden color.

Voilà! Your traditional Russian Oladyi are ready. Enjoy!
